Founder-Acharya

His Divine Grace

A.C. Bhaktivedante Swami Prabhupada

Srila Prabhupada is the spiritual ambassador who visited our planet to give us the message of Godhead and invite us to come back home – back to Godhead. We provide various lecture on different aspects of his teachings. However, we realize our limitation in capturing all the teachings of Srila Prabhupada in a few pages of a website. According to Srila Prabhupada, Krishna Consciousness is not an arm-chair philosophy or a part-time religion; it is a way of life. He presented the philosophy of Krishna Consciousness very extensively in his books and we request you to make it a habit to read Srila Prabhupada’s books and be benefited by them.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there any fees for these sessions ?

No, You only have to pay attention and gratitude to Krishna.

What will the Mode of these classes ?

Classes will be held in Online Mode.
Google Meet app will be used.

Can We switch to other Batch ?

Yes, You may join other batches.

How do I know right batch for me

1. You may consider SriMadBhagwatGita classes if you are beginning now and your age is above 8 years
2. You may consider SriMad Bhagwatam classes if you have spent at least 6 months in SriMadBhagwatGita class.
3. You may consider Bal Gopal class if your age is less than 8 years.

Why I should join ISKCON Gita Classes ?

Some Major reasons:
1. ISKCON strictly follows the guidelines of the Guru Parampara.
2. You will get Nice mentors in ISKCON.
3. ISKCON acharyas teaches you each and everything required for your advancement.

Why should I read SriMad Bhagwat Gita ?

Some Convincing Reasons are:
1. Bhagwat Gita tells us the message of Lord Krishna.
2. In Bhagwat Gita Arjun asked Lord Krishna
about each and every problem We may be facing.
3. Bhagwat Gita reminds us about our relationship with Lord Shri Krishna.
4. Krishna will be very happy when we will start reading Bhagwat Gita.
